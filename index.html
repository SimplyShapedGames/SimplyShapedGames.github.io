Advertisement

This paragraph explains in detail all advertisement related matters within Simply Shaped Games' games :

Simply Shaped Games uses Admob (by Google) as its main and only ad provider within its games.

The sole reason why Simply Shaped Games uses ads in its games, is as its main revenue provider.

Data Collection

Simply Shaped Games does not directly collect any data from its players, nor does it use any personal data it could acquire from its ad provider (Admob).
I simply look at how many and what type of ads have been showed and watched, and what revenue it has generated.

Admob does on the other hand collect data on players that watch or click on their ads through my games, but the player can easily decide (from the privacy popup in each of my games, located at launch or in the settings menu) to enable or disable personalized ads from Admob.
If the player does refuse receiving personalized ads, by playing Simply Shaped Games' games, the player accepts to receive non-personalized ads (for more details about non-personalized ads, click this link : https://support.google.com/admob/answer/7676680?hl=en).
For more details, please click on this link : https://policies.google.com/privacy
