Advertisement

This paragraph explains in detail all advertisement related matters within Simply Shaped Games' games :

Ad provider

Simply Shaped Games uses Admob (by Google) as its main and only ad provider within its games.
For more details about Admob, click on this link : https://admob.google.com/home/

Why Ads?

The sole reason why Simply Shaped Games uses ads in its games, is as its main revenue provider.
Please understand that I need a revenue to support my family, and because all my games are free to play, I show ads to my players in order to generate a revenue.

What type of ads?

The sole reason why Simply Shaped Games uses ads in its games, is as its main revenue provider.

- Passive ads :
(Passive ads are non voluntary ads that appear whilst the player is playing)
. Interstitials

- Rewarded ads :
(Rewarded ads are used to reward the player in exchange for them voluntarily watching a specific rewarded ad)
. Rewarded interstitials
. Rewarded videos

Data Collection

Simply Shaped Games does not directly collect any data from its players, nor does it use any personal data it could acquire from its ad provider (Admob).
I simply look at how many and what type of ads have been showed and watched, and what revenue it has generated.

Admob does on the other hand collect data on players that watch or click on their ads through my games, but the player can easily decide (from the privacy popup in each of my games, located at launch or in the settings menu) to enable or disable personalized ads from Admob.
If the player does refuse receiving personalized ads, by playing Simply Shaped Games' games, the player accepts to receive non-personalized ads (for more details about non-personalized ads, click this link : https://support.google.com/admob/answer/7676680?hl=en).
For more details, please click on this link : https://policies.google.com/privacy

Analytics

This paragraph explains in detail all Analytics related matters within Simply Shaped Games' games :

Analytics Service

Simply Shaped Games uses Unity's Analytics system as its main and only Analytics service within its games.
For more details about Analytics with Unity, click on this link : https://unity.com/features/analytics

Why Analytics?

The sole reason why Simply Shaped Games uses Analytics is to notice its games' flaws and deliver updates to fix them, allowing the players to enjoy the game more.

What type of Analytics?

In my games, I use several different non personal analytics calls, all solely related to the gameplay itself, some examples are :
On Player Death ; On Level Completed ; On Skin Unlocked ; etc...
(Simply to know if I should adjust a certain part of one of my games (difficulty, duration, etc...))

Data Collection

Simply Shaped Games does not directly collect any personal data from its players, nor does it use any personal data it could acquire from its Analytics service provider (Unity).
I simply look at how many players have played my games, how long for, plus all custom events (check above paragraph).

Unity does on the other hand collect data on players that play my games, but players can easily access (from the privacy popup in each of my games, located at launch or in the settings menu) all the information about their collected data, and also take several actions to affect the data in question (complete deletion, etc...).
For more details, please click on this link : https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy
